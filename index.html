Our Approach

At AurinkoLab, we create an encouraging, hands-on learning environment where children grow in skills and confidence. Our small group sizes (8-14 participants) ensure personalized attention for every child.

A short placement test on the first day helps us form balanced groups so each child can learn at their optimal pace.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do children need prior experience?

No prior experience is needed! Our curriculum is designed for all skill levels, and our placement test helps us group children appropriately.

What should my child bring?

Children should bring a water bottle, comfortable clothes, and closed-toe shoes. All technical equipment and materials are provided.

Are there any dietary accommodations?

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary restrictions. Please note any special requirements during registration.

What safety measures are in place?

All activities are supervised by trained staff. Safety equipment is provided for all technical activities, and our staff-to-child ratio ensures proper supervision.

What if my child needs to miss a day?

Our curriculum is designed to build day by day, but our instructors will help your child catch up if they miss a session.
